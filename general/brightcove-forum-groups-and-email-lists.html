Brightcove Forum Groups and Email Lists

In this topic, you will learn about the Brightcove forum groups and email lists that you can follow to stay informed about the latest solutions and release announcements.

Subscribe and follow

Brightcove offers the following forum groups and email lists:

Brightcove Player

Subscribe to the group to see player and associated plugin release announcements.

Group: https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/bcplayerdev

Email: bcplayerdev+subscribe@googlegroups.com

Brightcove Native Player SDKs

Subscribe to the group and join the forum. In the forum, you can ask technical questions and find answers from the Brightcove engineering team as well as your peers.

Group: https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/brightcove-native-player-sdks

Email: brightcove-native-player-sdks+subscribe@googlegroups.com

Brightcove Video Cloud Analytics

Subscribe to the group and join the forum. In the forum, you can ask technical questions and find answers from the Brightcove engineering team as well as your peers.

Group: https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/bc-aapi

Email: bc-aapi+subscribe@googlegroups.com
